## 🚨 COMMON REASONS EMAILS GO TO SPAM

### 1. **Content Triggers** (Check Your Email Content)

❌ **Spam Trigger Words** (Avoid these):
- Free, Act Now, Click Here, Limited Time, Urgent
- ALL CAPS SUBJECT LINES
- Too many exclamation marks!!!
- $$$, Make money, Earn money
- Viagra, Cialis, Pills (obviously)
- "Dear Friend", "You have won"

✅ **Professional Alternatives**:
- "Quick question about Reddit marketing"
- "Following up on our conversation"
- "RECHO - Your Reddit strategy session"

---

### 2. **Email Format Issues**

❌ **Problems That Trigger Spam Filters**:
- Email is mostly images (no text)
- Large attachments (>5MB)
- Multiple URL shorteners (bit.ly, tinyurl)
- Embedded forms or JavaScript
- Background colors in HTML emails
- Excessive links (>5 links)

✅ **Best Practices**:
- 60% text, 40% images (or less)
- Use full URLs (not shortened)
- Simple HTML or plain text
- Professional signature only
- 2-3 links maximum for initial outreach

---

### 3. **Sending Patterns**

❌ **Red Flags**:
- Sending 100+ emails immediately from new domain
- Sending to purchased/scraped lists
- High bounce rate (>5%)
- Low engagement (no opens/clicks)
- Blast emails to everyone at once

✅ **Good Sending Behavior**:
- Start with 5-10 emails/day (Week 1)
- Gradually increase to 20-30/day (Week 2)
- Only email people who expect it
- Personalize each email
- Allow time for replies/engagement
