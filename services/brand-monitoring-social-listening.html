What Are Reddit Brand Monitoring & Social Listening?

Your brand reputation is built in real-time on Reddit. Every mention, complaint, and conversation happens in public—and can spread instantly across 100,000+ communities.

Reddit brand monitoring is the practice of tracking every mention of your brand, products, competitors, and industry keywords across Reddit's 100,000+ active communities and 1.36 billion global users. We detect conversations in 5-15 minutes so you can respond before sentiment turns negative.

  • Brand mentions (company name, products, executives)
